Writing a Great Trend Report

  • Open with a strong hook — a bold statement or striking statistic about the trend.
  • Reference real runway shows, street style, or cultural moments that sparked the trend.
  • Explain why the trend is relevant right now, not just what it is.
  • Use specific designer names, collections, or cities to add authority.
  • Close with a forward-looking take — where is this trend heading next season?

Editorial Standards

FashionPulse publishes thoughtful, well-researched content. Before submitting, please ensure your article:

  • Is at least 200 words in length
  • Cites specific collections, designers, or events
  • Is original — not republished from another source
  • Is free from hate speech or discriminatory language
